# MKT-193: Biofield Energy Healing Based Vitamin D3 Effects on Bone Health

**Full Title:** Assessment of Biofield Energy Healing Based Vitamin D3 Effects on Bone Health Parameters Using Human Osteoblast Cell Line (MG-63)
**Authors:** Mahendra Kumar Trivedi, Alice Branton, Dahryn Trivedi, Gopal Nayak, Snehasis Jana
**Journal:** International Journal of Nutrition (ISSN: 2379-7835), Vol-3 Issue 1, 2018
**DOI:** 10.14302/issn.2379-7835.ijn-18-2301
**Read Date:** 2026-03-31

## Study Overview
**Type:** In vitro (cell line study)
**Subject:** Vitamin D3 treated with Biofield Energy, tested on MG-63 human bone osteosarcoma cell line
**Biofield Connection:** Demonstrates that Biofield Energy Treatment (The Trivedi Effect®) applied to vitamin D3 and DMEM can significantly enhance bone health parameters — ALP activity, collagen synthesis, and bone mineralization — showing consciousness can alter the biological activity of a nutrient.

## Methodology
- Cell line: MG-63 (human bone osteosarcoma cells)
- Growth medium: DMEM with 10% FBS
- Treatment: Biofield Energy by Mahendra Kumar Trivedi (~3 minutes, remote from USA to Dabur Research Foundation, New Delhi, India)
- Groups: UT-DMEM+UT-TI, UT-DMEM+BT-TI, BT-DMEM+UT-TI, BT-DMEM+BT-TI
- Positive control: Rutin hydrate
- Assays: MTT (cell viability), ALP activity, Collagen synthesis (Direct Red 80 / Sirius Red), Bone mineralization (Alizarin Red S staining)
- Statistics: One-way ANOVA with Dunnett's test, p≤0.05 significant

## Key Findings
1. MTT assay: Cell viability ranged 80%-121% across all groups — confirmed non-toxic at all concentrations up to 100 µg/mL
2. ALP activity significantly increased: 832.9% (UT-DMEM+BT-TI at 10 µg/mL), 209.4% (BT-DMEM+UT-TI at 10 µg/mL), 222.9% (BT-DMEM+BT-TI at 1 µg/mL) vs untreated
3. Collagen significantly increased: 487.7% (BT-DMEM+UT-TI at 100 µg/mL), 544.5% (BT-DMEM+BT-TI at 100 µg/mL), 116.2% (UT-DMEM+BT-TI at 1 µg/mL) vs untreated
4. Bone mineralization significantly increased: 344.9% (UT-DMEM+BT-TI at 50 µg/mL), 149.7% (BT-DMEM+UT-TI at 50 µg/mL), 183.6% (BT-DMEM+BT-TI at 100 µg/mL) vs untreated
5. Positive control (rutin) showed ALP increases of 80.90%, 58.70%, 35.80% at 1, 5, 10 µg/mL — Biofield treatment far exceeded this
