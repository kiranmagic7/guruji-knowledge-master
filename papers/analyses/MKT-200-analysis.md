# MKT-200: Enhancement of Hair Growth through Dermal Papilla Cell Stimulation

**Full Title:** Enhancement of Hair Growth through Stimulation of Dermal Papilla Cells in Human Follicular Dermal

**Authors:** Mahendra Kumar Trivedi, Snehasis Jana

**Journal:** Open Access Journal of Pharmaceutical Research, 2018, Volume 2, Issue 4

**DOI:** 10.23880/oajpr-16000166

**Read Date:** 2026-04-04

## Study Overview

**Type:** In vitro cellular study

**Subject:** Human follicular dermal papilla cells (HFDPC) grown in Biofield Energy Treated DMEM

**Biofield Connection:** Demonstrates The Trivedi Effect® applied to culture medium causing significant increase in dermal papilla cell proliferation—showing consciousness energy can affect hair follicle regeneration and hair growth processes.

## Methodology

**Instruments and Techniques:**
- BrdU (Bromodeoxyuridine) cell proliferation assay
- Statistical analysis: One-way ANOVA (p≤0.001 threshold for significance)
- Measurement of cell proliferation through BrdU incorporation

**Treatment Protocol:**
- Test item: DMEM (Dulbecco's Modified Eagle Medium)
- Three experimental groups:
  1. Untreated DMEM (control)
  2. BT-I: One-time Biofield Energy Treated DMEM
  3. BT-II: Two-times Biofield Energy Treated DMEM
- Biofield Energy Treatment by Mahendra Kumar Trivedi
- Cells incubated for 48 hours

**Positive Control:** Minoxidil at 0.001, 0.01, and 0.1 µM concentrations

## Key Findings

1. **One-time Biofield Treatment (BT-I): 14.8% increase** in dermal papilla cell proliferation vs untreated DMEM

2. **Two-times Biofield Treatment (BT-II): 124.3% increase** (p≤0.001) in dermal papilla cell proliferation vs untreated DMEM

3. **Positive control (Minoxidil) results:**
   - 0.001 µM: 68.57% increase
   - 0.01 µM: 187.14% increase
   - 0.1 µM: 230.95% increase

## Raw Data Points

**Dermal Papilla Cell (DPC) Proliferation:**
- Untreated DMEM: 100% (baseline)
- BT-I (one-time treatment): 114.8%
- BT-II (two-times treatment): 224.3% (p≤0.001)
- Minoxidil 0.001 µM: 168.57%
- Minoxidil 0.01 µM: 287.14%
- Minoxidil 0.1 µM: 330.95%

**Key observation:** Two applications of Biofield Energy Treatment showed cumulative/enhanced effect compared to single treatment.
