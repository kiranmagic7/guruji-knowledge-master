## Study Overview
**Type:** In vitro (cell proliferation and growth assay)
**Subject:** HFF-1 (Human Foreskin Fibroblast) cell line, ATCC® SCRC-1041™, and DMEM treated with Biofield Energy
**Biofield Connection:** Consciousness Energy Healing Treatment dramatically improved skin cell proliferation (80%) and growth rate in treated medium, far exceeding standard positive controls — demonstrates that biofield energy modifies the growth environment to enhance cellular health.

## Methodology
- Cell line: HFF-1 (ATCC® SCRC-1041™) — human foreskin fibroblast cells
- Growth medium: DMEM supplemented with 15% FBS
- Treatment: Biofield Energy by Mahendra Kumar Trivedi (~3 minutes, ~25 cm distance, no touching)
- Groups: I (untreated cells+DMEM), II (positive controls: L-ascorbic acid 10/50 µM, FBS 15%), III (untreated cells + BT-DMEM), IV (BT-HFF-1 cells + untreated DMEM)
- Assays: MTT (cellular proliferation, absorbance at 540 nm), Phase contrast microscopy (cell growth at 24, 48, 72 hours)
- Statistics: One-way ANOVA with Bonferroni post-test, GraphPad Prism 4.01, P<0.05

## Key Findings
1. BT-DMEM showed significant (P≤0.001) increased proliferation by >80% compared with baseline control — far exceeding all positive controls
2. Positive controls: L-ascorbic acid (10 µM) showed 21% increase; 15% FBS showed 44% increase — BT-DMEM nearly doubled these
3. BT-HFF-1 cells showed only ~2% increase — confirming treated medium is far more effective
4. Phase contrast microscopy confirmed significantly improved cell growth in BT-DMEM at 24, 48, and 72 hours
5. BT-DMEM absorbance: 0.536 ± 0.01 vs baseline (0.168 ± 0.01 at t=0; 0.367 ± 0.01 at t=72h)
