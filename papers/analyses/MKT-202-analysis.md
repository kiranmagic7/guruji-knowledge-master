# MKT-202: In Vivo Hair Growth in Mouse Skin

**Full Title:** Improvement of Hair Growth in Mouse Skin by Application of Consciousness Energy Treated Test Item

**Authors:** Trivedi MK, Jana S

**Journal:** Clinical Dermatology Open Access Journal, 2018, Volume 3, Issue 6

**DOI:** 10.23880/cdoaj-16000165

**Read Date:** 2026-04-04

## Study Overview

**Type:** In vivo animal study

**Subject:** Male C57BL/6 mice with prepared telogen skin; topical application of Biofield Energy Treated herbal extract mixture

**Test Item:** 1:1 ratio mixture of herbal extracts of **Phyllantus emblica** and **Eclipta alba**

**Biofield Connection:** Demonstrates The Trivedi Effect® applied to herbal extracts causing accelerated anagen induction, hair growth, and melanogenesis in living animals—showing consciousness energy can affect complex biological processes in vivo.

## Methodology

**Instruments and Techniques:**
- C57BL/6 mice telogen skin model (industry standard for hair growth research)
- Topical application to dorsal clipped skin
- Visual assessment of anagen induction and hair growth
- Skin biopsy for melanogenesis evaluation
- Hair growth scoring system

**Treatment Protocol:**
- Test item: 1:1 herbal extract mixture (Phyllantus emblica + Eclipta alba)
- Two groups:
  1. Untreated test item (no Biofield Energy Treatment)
  2. Biofield Energy Treated test item (by Mahendra Kumar Trivedi)
- Vehicle control group
- Positive control: Minoxidil sulphate

**Study Duration:** Monitored daily; hair growth observed from day 9-12 onward

## Key Findings

1. **Anagen induction and hair growth:**
   - **Untreated test item:** 50% (2 out of 4 animals showed hair growth)
   - **Biofield Energy Treated test item:** 100% (4 out of 4 animals showed hair growth)
   - **Positive control (Minoxidil):** 100% (4 out of 4 animals)
   - **Vehicle control:** 0% (no anagen induction or hair growth)

2. **Earlier anagen induction:**
   - **Biofield Energy Treated group:** Hair growth started on **day 9**
   - **Untreated group:** Hair growth started on **day 12**
   - **3-day acceleration** in anagen induction with Biofield Treatment

3. **Melanogenesis:**
   - **Both untreated and Biofield Energy Treated groups:** 100% melanogenesis after skin biopsy

## Raw Data Points

**Hair Growth Score (Visual Assessment):**
- Vehicle control: 0/4 animals (0%)
- Untreated test item: 2/4 animals (50%), onset day 12
- Biofield Energy Treated test item: 4/4 animals (100%), onset day 9
- Positive control (Minoxidil): 4/4 animals (100%)

**Anagen Induction:**
- Untreated: Visual anagen induction in all 4 animals, but only 50% progressed to hair growth
- Biofield Treated: Visual anagen induction in all 4 animals, 100% progressed to full hair growth

**Melanogenesis (Skin Biopsy):**
- Untreated test item: 100%
- Biofield Energy Treated test item: 100%

**Body Weight and General Health:**
- No adverse effects observed
- Normal weight gain in all groups
