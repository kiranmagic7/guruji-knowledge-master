# MKT-198: Effect of Consciousness Energy Healing on DMEM for Bone Health

**Full Title:** Effect of the Consciousness Energy Healing Treatment on DMEM for the Proliferation and Differentiation of Human Bone Osteosarcoma Cells to Improve Bone Health

**Authors:** Mahendra Kumar Trivedi, Alice Branton, Dahryn Trivedi, Gopal Nayak, Sambhu Charan Mondal, Snehasis Jana

**Journal:** Trends Journal of Sciences Research, 2018, Volume 3, Issue 3, 124-132

**DOI:** 10.31586/Biochemistry.0303.04

**Read Date:** 2026-04-04

## Study Overview

**Type:** In vitro cellular study

**Subject:** Human bone osteosarcoma cells - MG-63 (ATCC® CRL-1427™) grown in Biofield Energy Treated Dulbecco's Modified Eagle Medium (DMEM)

**Biofield Connection:** This study demonstrates The Trivedi Effect® (Biofield Energy Healing) applied to cell culture medium causing significant improvements in bone cell proliferation, alkaline phosphatase enzyme activity, and collagen synthesis—showing consciousness energy can affect cellular processes related to bone health and mineralization.

## Methodology

**Instruments and Techniques:**
- MTT assay for cell viability assessment (measured at 540 nm using Synergy HT microplate reader, BioTek, USA)
- ELISA-based assay for alkaline phosphatase (ALP) activity measurement (405 nm)
- Direct Sirius red dye binding assay for collagen synthesis quantification (540 nm)
- Statistical analysis: SigmaPlot Statistical Software (Version 11.0), one-way ANOVA with Dunnett's test post-hoc analysis

**Treatment Protocol:**
- Biofield Energy Treatment applied to DMEM culture medium for ~3 minutes from a distance of ~25 cm
- Energy transmission without physical contact
- Control group treated with sham healer (no knowledge of Biofield Energy Treatment)
- Cells incubated for 48 hours at 37°C, 5% CO2, 95% humidity

**Experimental Groups:**
- G1: Untreated medium (200 µL phenol-free DMEM + 10% CD-FBS)
- G2: Positive control (β-Estradiol + Biofield Treated medium + 10% CD-FBS) at 1, 10, and 100 nM
- G3: Biofield Energy Treated phenol-free DMEM + 10% CD-FBS

## Key Findings

1. **Cell viability increased by 24%** in the Biofield Energy Treated group (G3) compared to untreated cells (G1). Positive control (β-Estradiol) showed increases of 19%, 68%, and 53% at 1, 10, and 100 nM respectively vs G1.

2. **Alkaline phosphatase (ALP) activity increased by 72%** in the Biofield Energy Treated group (G3) compared to G1. Positive control showed increases of 38%, 35%, and 29% at 1, 10, and 100 nM respectively vs G1. (p≤0.001)

3. **Collagen synthesis increased by 19%** (195.1 ± 4.75 µg/mL in G3 vs 163.9 ± 2.31 µg/mL in G1, representing 119.0% of baseline). Positive control at 1 nM and 100 nM showed 119.3% and 119.0% of baseline respectively. (p≤0.001)
