# MKT-204: Assessment of Hair Growth Treatment with the Consciousness Energy Healing Treated Williams Medium E Using Mouse Vibrissae Hair Follicle Organ Culture

**Full Title:** Assessment of Hair Growth Treatment with the Consciousness Energy Healing Treated Williams Medium E Using Mouse Vibrissae Hair Follicle Organ Culture
**Authors:** Mahendra Kumar Trivedi, Snehasis Jana
**Journal:** Journal of Dermatological Research and Therapy, 2019 (received Dec 2018, accepted Jan 2019, published Jan 2019)
**DOI:** 10.14302/issn.2471-2175.jdrt-18-2520
**Read Date:** 2026-04-05

## Study Overview
**Type:** Ex vivo (vibrissae hair follicle organ culture)
**Subject:** Mouse vibrissae (whisker) hair follicles cultured in Williams Medium E
**Biofield Connection:** Demonstrates that The Trivedi Effect® applied to culture medium can promote hair follicle progression through the complete growth cycle (anagen → catagen → telogen), suggesting consciousness energy affects cellular differentiation and growth regulation.

## Methodology
- **Model System:** Mouse vibrissae hair follicles from 16-day-old C57BL/6 mice
- **Culture Medium:** Williams Medium E (phenol-free) supplemented with growth factors
- **Positive Control:** Minoxidil sulphate (1 mM)
- **Biofield Treatment:** Remote treatment (~3 minutes) by Mahendra Kumar Trivedi to Williams Medium E only (not cells)
- **Control Group:** Sham healer treatment (healer with no knowledge of Biofield Energy Healing)
- **Observation Period:** 7 days (daily monitoring)
- **Measurement Tool:** UTHSCSA Image tool version 3
- **Study Parameters:**
  1. Hair bulb thickness (mm)
  2. Telogen follicle formation (% of follicles showing hair shaft displacement)

## Key Findings

### Experimental Groups:
1. **Group 1 (Untreated control):** Williams Medium E with growth factors (no treatment)
2. **Group 2:** Biofield Energy Treated Williams Medium E
3. **Group 3 (Positive control):** Minoxidil sulphate (1 mM)

### Critical Findings:

1. **Untreated group:** Showed increased bulb thickness (catagen-like changes) but **NO telogen formation** through day 7
2. **Biofield Treated group:** Showed **71% telogen formation** by day 7, demonstrating complete hair cycle progression
3. **Telogen hallmark:** Shifting of hair shaft from original position observed in 5/7 follicles (71%) in treated group
4. **Medium vs. cell treatment (consistent with MKT-203):** Treatment applied to culture medium was effective; cells were not directly treated

## Raw Data Points
- Untreated bulb thickness: Day 1 = 1.5 ± 0.45 mm, Day 5 = 1.8 ± 0.57 mm, Day 7 = 1.9 ± 0.60 mm
- Minoxidil bulb thickness: Day 1 = 1.9 ± 0.29 mm, Day 5 = 2.6 ± 0.37 mm, Day 7 = 3.3 ± 0.36 mm
- Biofield Treated telogen formation: Day 3 = 43%, Day 5 = 57%, Day 7 = 71%
- Untreated telogen formation: 0% through day 7
- Statistical significance: p ≤ 0.05

## Hair Growth Cycle Background (from paper)
**Anagen (growing phase):** Active hair growth, cell proliferation in hair matrix  
**Catagen (regression phase):** Intermediate stage, hair follicle regresses  
**Telogen (resting phase):** No cell proliferation, hair shaft shifts/sheds  
**Exogen (shedding phase):** Hair is released

**Normal progression:** Anagen → Catagen → Telogen → Exogen → (new cycle begins)

The Trivedi Effect® successfully drove follicles through this complete cycle, while untreated follicles remained stuck in early phases.

## Notable Methodology Details
- **Mice strain:** C57BL/6 (standard research strain)
- **Mouse age:** 16 days old (optimal for vibrissae isolation)
