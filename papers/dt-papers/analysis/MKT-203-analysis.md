**Authors:** Mahendra Kumar Trivedi, Alice Branton, Dahryn Trivedi, Gopal Nayak, Mayank Gangwar, Snehasis Jana
**Journal:** Investigative Dermatology and Venereology Research, 2018

**Read Date:** 2026-04-05

## Study Overview
**Type:** In vitro (scratch assay)
**Subject:** HFF-1 cells (Human Foreskin Fibroblast) and DMEM (Dulbecco's Modified Eagle Medium)
**ATCC Number:** ATCC® SCRC-1041™ (HFF-1 cell line)
**Biofield Connection:** Demonstrates that Consciousness Energy Healing (The Trivedi Effect®) can enhance wound healing through cell culture media (DMEM) rather than direct cell treatment, showing consciousness affecting biological repair mechanisms.

## Methodology
- **Assay Type:** Scratch assay (in vitro wound healing model)
- **Cell Line:** HFF-1 (human foreskin fibroblast) from ATCC SCRC-1041™
- **Culture Medium:** DMEM supplemented with 15% FBS, penicillin (100 U/mL), streptomycin (100 μg/mL)
- **Positive Control:** Recombinant Human Epidermal Growth Factor (Hu EGF) at 30 ng/mL
- **Biofield Treatment:** Remote treatment (~3 minutes, ~25 cm distance, no physical contact) by Mahendra Kumar Trivedi
- **Measurement Tool:** WimScratch Image analysis software
- **Observation Period:** 24 hours post-scratch
- **Growth Conditions:** 37°C, 5% CO₂, 95% humidity

## Key Findings

### Experimental Groups:
1. **Group I (Baseline control):** Cells in untreated DMEM
2. **Group II (Positive control):** Cells in DMEM with Hu-EGF (30 ng/mL)
3. **Group III:** HFF-1 cells in Biofield Energy Treated DMEM
4. **Group IV:** Biofield Energy Treated HFF-1 cells in untreated DMEM

### Quantitative Results (24-hour observation):

| Treatment Group | % Scratched Area | % Migration |
|-----------------|------------------|-------------|
| Baseline control | 5.6 | 0 |
| EGF (30 ng/mL) | 2.0 | 64.3 |
| **Biofield Treated DMEM** | **2.7** | **51.8** |
| Biofield Treated HFF-1 cells | 12.0 | -114.3 (no migration) |

### Key Numerical Data:
1. **Biofield Treated DMEM:** 51.8% fibroblast migration (vs 0% baseline), 2.7% scratch area (vs 5.6% baseline) — **scratch area reduced by 2.9 percentage points**
2. **Positive control (EGF):** 64.3% migration, 2.0% scratch area
3. **Biofield Treated cells:** No migration observed, scratch area increased to 12% (worse than baseline)

### Critical Observation:
**The Trivedi Effect® was significantly more effective when applied to the DMEM culture medium rather than directly to the cells.** Treated medium showed 51.8% migration rate (80% as effective as EGF), while treated cells showed no migration and increased wound area.
