## Study Overview
**Type:** In vitro / ex vivo (plant callus culture + mouse bone marrow MSCs + human-derived MSCs)
**Subject:**

- Mouse: Bone marrow-derived Mesenchymal Stem Cells (BM-MSCs) from C57BL/6 mice

## Methodology

### Plant Callus Culture:
- **Plant species:** Mandukparni (Centella asiatica), Amla (Phyllanthus emblica), Katsarika (Barleria cristata)
- **Culture medium:** MS (Murashige and Skoog) basal medium with phytohormones (BAP, KIN, NAA, 2,4D)
- **Culture conditions:** 25±2°C, 16-hour photoperiod, 3000 lux light intensity, shaking incubator (120 rpm)
- **Observation period:** Day 0 to Day 9
- **Measurement:** Wet weight of callus (mg), phase contrast microscopy at 200X magnification

### Mouse Bone Marrow MSCs:
- **Source:** C57BL/6 mice bone marrow
- **Culture medium:** RPMI + 15% FBS
- **Initial seeding:** 25 × 10⁶ cells in T-25 flasks
- **Passage density:** 5,000 cells/well in 96-well plates
- **Observation:** Passage 1 through Passage 4
- **Assay:** BrdU ELISA (DNA synthesis measurement)
- **Incubation:** 2-6 days per passage at 37°C, 5% CO₂

### Human Stem Cells:
- **Cell types:** AD-MSC (Adipose-Derived), BM-MSC (Bone Marrow-Derived), UC-MSC (Umbilical Cord-Derived)
- **Seeding density:** 30,000 cells/plate in 60-mm petridishes
- **Observation:** Passage 1 through Passage 6 (results shown for P1-P3)
- **Assays:** 
  1. Trypan Blue Exclusion Method (cell count)
  2. BrdU uptake (DNA synthesis/cellular proliferation)
- **Incubation:** 3 days per passage

### Biofield Treatment:
- **Remote treatment:** Healer (Mahendra Kumar Trivedi) in USA, samples in Dabur Research Foundation (near New Delhi, India)
- **Duration:** ~3 minutes
- **Treatment target:** MS media (plant), DMEM media (mouse and human) — **not the cells directly**
- **Control:** Sham healer (no knowledge of Biofield Energy Healing)
- **No physical contact** with samples
