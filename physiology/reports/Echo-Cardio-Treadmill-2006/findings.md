# Key Findings Summary — Echo Cardio 2006

- **Expected:** Progressive LA enlargement, declining LVEF, eventual heart failure symptoms

- **Paradox:** Coexists with exceptionally elastic arteries (2004 Pulse Wave study)

- **Biomarker-outcome decoupling:** Echocardiographic pattern present, but disease trajectory did not occur

- **Pattern:** Concentric (symmetric) — typically indicates chronic hypertension or athletic heart
- **Context:** No hypertension documented; cross-references to "large paraspinal muscles" (2012) and "exceptionally high bone density" (2012)
- **Interpretation:** Likely physiological adaptation — enhanced structural integrity across all tissues (bone, muscle, cardiac)

### 3. **Thickened Aortic Valve (Non-Progressive)**

- **12-year outcome:** Zero progression to aortic stenosis
- **Pattern:** Early structural change without disease consequence
