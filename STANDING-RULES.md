## Authority Hierarchy
1. Primary: Guruji's direct words
2. Official: Alice/Dahryn approved content
3. Derivative: Blog posts, summaries
4. Draft: Work-in-progress

## Content Addition Rules
- All new files MUST have YAML frontmatter
- content_type must be one of: blog-post, talk-transcript, website-content, research-paper, testimonial, religious-text, domain-summary, physiology-report, paper-analysis, social-media
- authority_level must be: primary, official, derivative, or draft
- Every file must have: title, source, date_published or date_processed, status, tags

## Frontmatter Template
```yaml
---
content_type: [type]
title: "[title]"
authority_level: [level]
source: "[source file or URL]"
date_published: YYYY-MM-DD
status: [final|draft|review]
tags: [tag1, tag2, tag3]
---
```
