## **Divine Energy Is Without Loss Or Decay**

Stand next to a lamp. The light is bright. Walk away, and it dims. Walk far enough, and the light is gone. The lamp still burns. But its energy has faded with distance until none of it reaches you.

Every energy in the known universe behaves this way. Electricity, magnetism, heat, light, radio waves. They all travel outward, weaken as they go, and eventually disappear. Science observed this so consistently that it became a law. Energy decays with distance. Everything fades. Everything dies.

In 2022, this law cracked.

Three scientists won the Nobel Prize for proving that two particles, once linked, stay linked no matter how far apart you pull them. Measure one, and the other responds instantly. No signal travels between them. No medium carries anything. No energy is lost. The connection does not weaken, whether the particles are inches apart or galaxies apart. Einstein called this impossible. Thousands of experiments proved it real.

Science had found the first crack in the law of decay. But the ancient seers had walked through that crack thousands of years before.

The Mundaka Upanishad, written millennia before any laboratory existed, says: \"The sun does not shine there, nor the moon and the stars, nor these lightnings, not to speak of this fire. When He shines, everything shines after Him. By His light everything is lighted.\"

This is not describing a brighter lamp. It is describing the source from which all light borrows its shine. A light that does not travel because it is already everywhere. A light that does not weaken because distance was never a limitation.

The Bhagavad Gita says the same. The divine pervades all beings and all of creation. Not as a force that moves from place to place, but as a presence that simply is. Everywhere. Always. Fully. Without diminishing.

This is what Guruji teaches. Divine Energy is consciousness, and it is not subject to the rules that govern every other energy in the universe. It does not travel because it is already at every destination. It does not weaken because time and distance do not apply to it. It does not require a medium because nothing carries it. It does not miss its target because consciousness does not scatter. It arrives precisely where it is intended, every time.

Quantum entanglement is science catching up to what the ancient texts always knew. But it is a glimpse through a keyhole. It works only between paired particles in specific conditions. Consciousness has no such limitation. It is not a connection between two things. It is the ground beneath everything. It reaches every heart, every home, every corner of this earth at the same moment. It loses nothing on the way, because it is not traveling. It is already there.

Everything decays. Except one thing. Divine Energy, expressed as consciousness, is without loss or decay. It always has been. It always will be.
